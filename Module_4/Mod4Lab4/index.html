Eight of the most famous artists!

NavBar
Botticelli

Alessandro di Mariano Filipepi, known as Botticelli, was one of the most representative artists of Florentine culture

Michelangelo

Master sculptor and painter, famous for David and the Sistine Chapel ceiling

Leonardo da Vinci

The Renaissance genius behind Mona Lisa and The Last Supper

Claude Monet

The father of Impressionism, best known for his Water Lilies series

Salvador Dalí

Surrealist icon famous for The Persistence of Memory

Vincent van Gogh

Known for his expressive brushstrokes in Starry Night and Sunflowers

Pablo Picasso

A pioneer of Cubism, creating works like Guernica and Les Demoiselles d’Avignon

Johannes Vermeer

Dutch painter celebrated for Girl with a Pearl Earring
